Who can apply?

Artists and programmers working with or interested in working with hydra video synth (as well as live coding, video art, open-source technology) are welcome to submit an application. Prior experience with coding or specifically with hydra is not necessary.

How many grants will be awarded?

We will award up to six grants.

Can I apply as a group/collective?

Yes! Please note that the awarded amount will still be $200 USD regardless of the size of the group/collecitve.

How is it funded?

The grant is funded by donations to the hydra open collective. The more funds we receive, the more awards we are able to offer in the future. Please support if you are able!

When is the deadline

The deadline for applications is December 2nd at 23:59 GMT. Late applications will not be accepted.

Who will decide who gets the grants

Each application will be judged by the core Hydra Grants team and an invited guest judge. Learn more about the team here.

Questions

If you have any questions for the team please check the FAQ or get in touch at hydra.video.synth@gmail.com. Please remember that we are a team of volunteers so it may take a moment for us to respond.
